.. py:class:: ParsePlus(pattern, extraTypes=None, escapeSequences=None, caseSensitive=True)

   use "{s1} and {s2}" or "{} and {}" if the contents can differ.
   escapeSequences such as backslash-escapes (see examples in tests).
