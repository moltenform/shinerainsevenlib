srss.strToList(s, replaceComments=True)¶

When quickly writing code, get a list of strings, useful for short scripts.

>>> from shinerainsevenlib.standard import *
>>> # (this will import srss)
>>>
>>> srss.strToList('''
>>>   many
>>>   lines
>>>   of
>>>   text''')
['many', 'lines', 'of', 'text']

srss.easyToEnterFilepath(s, checkIfExists=True)¶

When writing a quick script, you’ll often copy a filepath and paste it in. Sometimes that filepath will have quotes around it, or sometimes there will be newlines. If you use easyToEnterFilepath, it will 1) remove quotes and extra whitespace 2) confirm that the path exists 3) also support commenting out lines with #.
